Customer Care

This Policy applies to information collected by or on behalf of Fresh through this Website, and through our Fresh Family Rewards Program (the “Membership”) available at our retail store locations. Privacy & Safety

We have adopted appropriate technical and organizational measures that are designed to protect your data against loss, misuse, alteration, destruction or unauthorized access.
