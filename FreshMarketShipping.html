Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

Once your order is placed it will be reviewed. Fresh reserves the right to cancel any orders that are not in line with our Terms and Conditions. We apologize, but currently we are unable to process and ship orders to destinations outside of the United States. Business Days: Monday - Friday, excluding federal holidays within the United States, as well as the day after Thanksgiving. Orders received by 11am EST on business days will be shipped the same day. Orders made after 11am EST will ship the following business day. Orders placed during the weekend will be processed on the next business day.

If you have a question about the status of your order, please reference the tracking number in your confirmation email and call us at 800-373-7420. Please allow 24 business hours to receive a response. We can not deliver shipments to PO Boxes. Also please note that Fresh cannot ship perfumes to APO/FPO addresses as we can only ship to these addresses using United States Postal Service. We thank you for your understanding.

Return & Exchange Policy

For purchases made at a Fresh store, when returned within 60 days of our shops re-opening, an exchange, store credit or a refund will be issued when accompanied by a receipt. Refunds will only be issued back into original form of payment. For purchases made on fresh.com, when physically returned within 60 days of our shops re-opening, an exchange or a store credit (store credit to be used at a physical Fresh shop only), will be issued when accompanied by a receipt. The shipping confirmation email issued with purchases made on fresh.com is considered the original receipt. For purchases in the UK, this return policy does not affect your statutory rights.

When your fresh.com order arrives, please inspect the package for any damage. It is normal for the shipping package to show some wear, however, if you think a product in your shipment is damaged, please contact us immediately at returns@fresh.com or 1-800-373-7420. Please provide the order number along with your email address and phone number for fastest service. To ensure prompt resolution, please keep the original shipping package and the damaged product for inspection. If, after having examined the product, we confirm that it is damaged, we will provide you instructions for returning the damaged product to us at our expense and issue a refund for the price you paid for the product to the original purchaserâ€™s method of payment. Your original shipping and handling charges will not be refunded, but any replacement for a damaged or defective product will be shipped to you free of charge.
